Two crowds. Non-developers who want to publish what they're building with an AI assistant, without learning ssh, security groups, and dashboards. And builders who want their multi-agent systems to self-deploy the apps they generate.
We're MCP-native. The first VM provider that lets your agent provision infra by chatting. There's no brand tax: you pay for compute, full stop. No egress markup. In practice that lands ~70% below the hyperscalers.
Dedicated AMD EPYC vCPUs, NVMe-backed storage with no throttling, and unlimited egress across global regions. You get an SSH key, a public IP, and a URL on huddle01.app.
Anything that speaks Model Context Protocol: Claude Code, Claude Desktop, Cursor, Antigravity, Windsurf, Zed, Continue. If your tool supports MCP, it supports Huddle01 VMs.
Per second. The moment your VM stops, billing stops. No idle charges, no minimum commitment, no egress surprises.
Yes. console.huddle01.com gives you the full UI, and there's a CLI for scripts and CI. The MCP server is the third path. Pick whichever fits the moment.
